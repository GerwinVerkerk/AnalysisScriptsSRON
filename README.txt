The scripts included in this repository were used and made during the graduation internship of Gerwin Verkerk at SRON.
This internship started on 4th of February 2019 and ended on the 31th of May 2019.

The scripts were made to analyse data obtained from the XFDM measurement setup and to make plots out of them.

The scripts made by Gerwin Verkerk are the following:
LatestAnalysisScript.py
PickPointTransition.py
CalibrateNewIVfromOld.py
RVcomparer.py


Scripts which are needed as dependencies on these scripts:
Calibrate_HDFIV.py
Calibrate_IV_TXT.py
pars_run78.py
calivac_niceplot_pwr.py
